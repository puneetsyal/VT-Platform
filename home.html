Programs


Programs are groups of projects, initiatives or devops activity.


Programs build capabilities for the platform, based on business value, user need and technology strategy.


Every program needs to consider:

Are any "view and analyze" capabilities needed as part of the program?

Are any "act and transact" capabilities needed?

Are there any integrations into exterla tools which need to be built or optimized?

Platform


The platform is the repository of stockbroking capabilities, organized per the Platform Structure.


Underlying these capabilities is an infrastructure which supplies these capabilties with data, processing, security, content, digital identity and other services.

Products


Products bring together various capabilities into a single package. This package is branded with the name of the product.


Products are not static. Capabilties inside a product change and evolve depedning on cotracts with the clients who use the product. This is systematically controlled through the wrapper, which is used to determine which capabilities are put into a product.


In addition, capabilities are also sold individually as APIs and portable UIs.
